ACTIVITIES

Guided and self-guided activities include diving, hiking,visits to the botanic gardens,sunrise and sunset feeding of dolphins and walks on the beach as well as the spa.


Spa

Indulge in signature spa rituals and relax in the sanctuary that is Essentia Day Spa.Emerge feeling rejuvenated, refreshed and relaxed.

Botanic gardens

Explore and experience the vegetation and witness the variety and diversity of the botanical life, all of which form part of the Sunflower heritage.

Beach

Take enjoyable and relaxing walks along the beach as well as swim in the beach. It is childe friendly and there's so much more to do like build sand castles,sunbath and so on.


Diving

Sunflower Island is located on the Sunflower Reef with world famous dive site

The diversity of the dive locations within the waters surrounding the island ensure that those with all levels of diving experience can explore and experience the crystal clear waters and witness the variety

Hiking

Experience the nature of the island on a guided walk to the Sunflower Mountain to see the view.

Dolphins

Experience the most picturesque reef and marine life by simply feeding a dolphin
