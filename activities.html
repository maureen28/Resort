Botanic gardens

Explore and experience the vegetation and witness the variety and diversity of the botanical life, all of which form part of the Sunflower heritage.

Beach

Take enjoyable and relaxing walks along the beach as well as swim in the beach. It is childe friendly and there's so much more to do like build sand castles,sunbath and so on.

Diving

Sunflower Island is located on the Sunflower Reef with world famous dive site

The diversity of the dive locations within the waters surrounding the island ensure that those with
all levels of diving experience can explore and experience the crystal clear waters and witness the variety
and diversity of the marine life, all of which form part of the heritage list.

All of our dive sites are under protection of the Sunflower Marine Park Authority.

Our experienced marine team is passionate and educated about the Sunflower Reef and the diversity and uniqueness of its plant and marine life.

The team will provide a thorough briefing and commentary en route to your chosen site and will be on hand to assist with
any questions or guidance you may require to ensure your experience is everything you dreamed of.
